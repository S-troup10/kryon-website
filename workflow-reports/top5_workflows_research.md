Top 5 Business Automation Workflows — Research

Summary

This document describes five high-impact, sellable business automation workflows: Lead qualification, Customer onboarding, Invoice & payments automation, Support ticket triage & routing, and Employee onboarding & HR automation. For each workflow: description, typical pricing, estimated monthly run cost, integrations, architecture diagram (see diagrams/), setup time, typical ROI, target customers, and example vendors.

1) Lead Qualification
Description: Automate inbound lead capture, enrichment, scoring, and routing to sales reps.
Typical price: $200–$1,500/month per workflow (tiered by volume and integrations).
Estimated cost to run: $20–$200/month (API credits, enrichment costs, small compute).
Integrations: CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce), webforms, Clearbit/ZoomInfo, email, Zapier/Make, Slack.
Setup time: 1–5 days (template + CRM mapping).
ROI: Faster response times, higher conversion rates; break-even often within weeks.
Examples: Outreach sequences, HubSpot workflows, Clearbit + Zapier stacks.
Diagram: diagrams/lead_qualification.svg

2) Customer Onboarding
Description: Automate user/account setup, welcome communications, tutorial flows, and initial configuration.
Typical price: $300–$2,000/month or per-seat onboarding fee.
Estimated cost to run: $30–$300/month.
Integrations: Product API, CRM, email provider, docs (Notion/Docs), billing.
Setup time: 3–14 days (depends on product integrations).
ROI: Reduced churn, improved activation metrics.
Examples: Intercom onboarding flows, product-driven onboarding orchestrations.
Diagram: diagrams/customer_onboarding.svg

3) Invoice & Payments Automation
Description: Generate invoices from orders, send reminders, reconcile payments, and create accounting entries.
Typical price: $250–$1,200/month.
Estimated cost to run: $10–$150/month (depends on transaction volumes).
Integrations: Stripe/PayPal, QuickBooks/Xero, invoicing templates, email.
Setup time: 2–7 days.
ROI: Reduced DSO, fewer late payments, lower accounting overhead.
Examples: Stripe + QuickBooks automations, Bill.com workflows.
Diagram: diagrams/invoice_payments.svg

4) Support Ticket Triage & Routing
Description: Auto-categorize, prioritize, and route support tickets; suggest KB articles; trigger SLA timers.
Typical price: $200–$1,000/month.
Estimated cost to run: $20–$300/month (NLP API costs).
Integrations: Zendesk/Freshdesk, Slack, email, Knowledge Base (Confluence/Notion), LLM APIs.
Setup time: 2–10 days.
ROI: Faster resolution, lower support headcount required.
Examples: Zendesk automations, Semantic search + routing layers.
Diagram: diagrams/support_triage.svg

5) Employee Onboarding & HR Automation
Description: Automate new hire paperwork, provisioning, training assignments, and access control.
Typical price: $300–$2,500/month (per-employee pricing models exist).
Estimated cost to run: $10–$200/month (per active hiring cadence).
Integrations: HRIS (BambooHR), G Suite/Office365, Slack, Okta, payroll systems.
Setup time: 3–14 days.
ROI: Faster time-to-productivity, fewer provisioning errors, better compliance.
Examples: BambooHR + Zapier + Okta flows, HRIS-driven provisioning.
Diagram: diagrams/hr_onboarding.svg

Notes on pricing and costs
- Pricing ranges vary by complexity, SLA, and number of integrations. Offer tiered pricing: Starter, Business, Enterprise.
- Run costs depend on LLM/API usage, webhook volume, and storage. Provide transparent cost estimates and margins.
